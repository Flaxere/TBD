HOW TO PLAY

The number to be guessed must be a 4 digit number, using only digits from 1 - 9

No digit is allowed to be repeated

For every guess that the player makes, he gets 2 values - the number of bulls and the number of cows

1 bull means the guess and target number have 1 digit in common, and in the correct position.

1 cow means the guess and the target have 1 digit in common , but not in correct position.

e.g. Let the target be 1234. Guessing 4321 will give 0 bulls and 4 cows. 3241 will give 1 bull and 3 cows.

The player gets 10 chances to correctly guess the entire number.
